Overview

What Is Invisalign?

😁

Clear Aligners

Invisalign uses a series of custom-made, BPA-free transparent plastic aligners that fit snugly over your teeth. Each set gradually shifts your teeth into the desired position with precision and comfort.

🔄

Progressive Treatment

You wear each set of aligners for approximately 1–2 weeks before progressing to the next. Each aligner is slightly different, gently and consistently moving your teeth toward the final outcome.

📱

3D Treatment Planning

Advanced 3D digital scanning and software map out your entire tooth movement journey before treatment begins — so you can even preview your final smile before a single aligner is worn.

✨

Teen & Adult Options

Invisalign offers tailored solutions for both teenagers and adults. Whether correcting mild crowding or more complex alignment issues, there is an Invisalign plan designed for your needs.

Candidacy

Who Is Invisalign For?

🦷

Crowded Teeth

When teeth overlap due to lack of space in the jaw, Invisalign aligners gradually create room and move teeth into proper positions for a balanced, even smile.

↔️

Gaps Between Teeth

Spaces or gaps between teeth can affect both aesthetics and function. Invisalign gently closes these gaps, improving the look of your smile and preventing food trapping.

⚙️

Overbite or Underbite

Misaligned bites — where upper or lower teeth protrude — can cause jaw pain and uneven wear. Invisalign corrects these bite issues to restore proper function and comfort.

🔀

Crossbite

When upper and lower teeth don't meet correctly side to side, it can lead to chipping and gum issues. Invisalign realigns the bite to prevent further damage.

😔

Self-Conscious About Braces

Adults and teens who want straighter teeth but are concerned about the appearance of metal braces will find Invisalign's virtually invisible aligners a confidence-friendly alternative.

🎯

Post-Braces Relapse

If your teeth have shifted after previous orthodontic treatment, Invisalign can efficiently realign them back to their corrected position without the need for full braces again.

Why Choose Us

Benefits of Invisalign at MCR

👁️

Virtually Invisible

The clear aligners are nearly undetectable when worn, allowing you to straighten your teeth without anyone noticing — ideal for professional and social settings.

🍽️

Removable for Eating & Cleaning

Unlike fixed braces, Invisalign aligners can be removed when eating, drinking, brushing, and flossing — so there are no food restrictions and oral hygiene is effortless.

😌

Comfortable & Smooth

Made from smooth, flexible plastic with no metal wires or brackets, Invisalign aligners are gentle on cheeks and gums and cause far less irritation than traditional braces.

⏱️

Fewer Clinic Visits

Invisalign requires fewer in-clinic check-ups than traditional braces. You simply progress through your aligner sets at home, visiting us approximately every 6–8 weeks.

📊

Predictable Digital Results

3D treatment planning technology lets Dr. Ramamurthy Reddy map out each stage of your tooth movement precisely, giving you a clear picture of what to expect at every step.

❤️

Improved Oral Health

Straighter teeth are easier to clean properly. Correcting misalignment reduces the risk of gum disease, tooth decay, and abnormal enamel wear caused by an improper bite.

Step by Step

The Invisalign Process at MCR

1

Initial Consultation

Dr. Ramamurthy Reddy assesses your teeth, bite, and overall oral health to determine whether Invisalign is the right solution for you and discusses your smile goals in detail.

2

3D Digital Scanning

A precise digital scan of your teeth is taken — no messy impressions. This scan is used to create a detailed 3D model of your mouth and your personalised treatment plan.

3

Custom Aligner Fabrication

Your unique series of clear aligners is manufactured to exact specifications. Each aligner in the series represents a stage of your tooth movement towards the final position.

4

Fitting Your First Aligners

You receive your first sets of aligners and a full briefing on how to wear, remove, and care for them. Small tooth-coloured attachments may be bonded to some teeth to aid movement.

5

Wearing & Progressing

You wear your aligners for 20–22 hours per day, switching to the next set every 1–2 weeks. Progress check-ups at MCR every 6–8 weeks ensure everything is on track.

6

Retention & Final Result

Once your final aligner is complete, a custom retainer is provided to hold your teeth in their new position and protect your beautiful results long-term.

Care & Safety

Aftercare & What to Expect

⏰

Wear Aligners Consistently

For best results, wear your aligners for 20–22 hours each day. Removing them for longer periods can slow your progress and affect the accuracy of your treatment plan.

🪥

Clean Aligners Daily

Rinse your aligners with lukewarm water and brush them gently with a soft toothbrush each day. Avoid hot water as it can warp the plastic and affect the fit.

🚫

Remove Before Eating or Drinking

Always remove your aligners before consuming anything other than plain water. Food and coloured drinks can stain and damage the aligners and compromise your oral hygiene.

📅

Wear Your Retainer

After completing treatment, wearing your retainer as directed is essential to maintain your new smile. Long-term retainer wear prevents teeth from gradually drifting back.

Invisalign is suitable for most adults and teenagers with mild to moderate alignment issues including crowding, gaps, overbites, underbites, and crossbites. Severe or complex orthodontic cases may still require traditional braces. Dr. Ramamurthy Reddy will assess your suitability during a comprehensive consultation and recommend the most appropriate treatment for your specific needs.

Invisalign is generally much more comfortable than traditional metal braces. You may feel mild pressure or slight discomfort for the first day or two after switching to a new set of aligners — this is a sign the aligners are working. There are no sharp brackets or wires to irritate your mouth, making the overall experience considerably more comfortable.

Treatment duration varies depending on the complexity of your case. Minor corrections may take as little as 6 months, while more involved cases typically take 12–18 months. On average, most adults complete Invisalign treatment within 12–15 months. Your personalised timeline will be outlined clearly during your initial consultation and 3D treatment planning session.

Some patients notice a very slight lisp during the first few days of wearing a new set of aligners. This is entirely temporary and typically resolves within a few days as your tongue adjusts to the aligners. The vast majority of patients find they can speak comfortably and naturally within a short adjustment period.
