Overview

What Are Dental Implants?

🦷

Titanium Root

A small titanium post is placed into your jawbone, acting exactly like a natural tooth root — stable, strong, and bio-compatible.

🔩

Abutment

A connector piece (abutment) is attached to the implant, which holds and supports the visible crown securely in place.

✨

Custom Crown

A natural-looking ceramic crown is placed on top — matched to the shade of your surrounding teeth for a seamless smile.

🔒

Permanent Result

Unlike dentures or bridges, implants fuse with your jawbone through osseointegration — making them a permanent, lifelong solution.

Candidacy

Who Needs Dental Implants?

🦷

Missing One or More Teeth

If you've lost a tooth due to decay, trauma, or extraction and want a permanent replacement.

😣

Uncomfortable Dentures

Patients who find removable dentures unstable, painful, or embarrassing during eating and speaking.

🦴

Preventing Bone Loss

After tooth loss, the jawbone shrinks. Implants stimulate the bone just like natural roots, stopping this process.

😁

Smile Aesthetics

Gaps in your smile affect appearance and confidence. Implants restore a full, natural-looking smile.

🍽️

Difficulty Chewing

Struggling to eat hard or crunchy foods because of missing teeth? Implants restore full chewing power.

🗣️

Speech Problems

Missing front teeth can cause a lisp or slurred speech. Implants restore normal speech patterns.

Why Choose Implants

Benefits of Dental Implants

♾️

Lifelong Solution

With proper care, implants can last a lifetime — far longer than bridges or dentures.

🌿

No Damage to Adjacent Teeth

Unlike bridges, implants don't require grinding down healthy neighbouring teeth for support.

🪥

Easy Maintenance

Brush and floss normally — no special soaking or adhesives needed like with dentures.

🎯

Natural Feel

Patients consistently rate implants as the closest replacement to their natural teeth in feel and function.

💪

Restores Bite Force

Regain up to 100% of your natural chewing force — eat whatever you want with confidence.

😊

Boosts Confidence

A complete, stable smile improves your self-esteem in social and professional situations.

Step by Step

The Implant Process at MCR

1

Initial Consultation & X-Ray

Dr. Ramamurthy Reddy examines your mouth, reviews digital X-rays and CBCT scans to assess bone density and plan the exact implant position.

2

Treatment Planning

A personalised implant plan is created — number of implants, type of crown, timeline, and cost are discussed transparently.

3

Implant Placement (Surgery)

Under local anaesthesia, the titanium implant is placed into the jawbone. The procedure is minimally invasive and virtually painless.

4

Healing & Osseointegration

Over 2–4 months, the implant fuses with your jawbone. Most patients resume normal activity within 1–2 days post-surgery.

5

Abutment Placement

Once healed, a small connector piece is attached to the implant. Impressions are taken to fabricate your custom crown.

6

Crown Fitting

Your natural-looking ceramic crown is placed and fine-tuned for perfect bite alignment. Walk out with your complete smile!

Care & Safety

Aftercare & What to Expect

🩹

Mild Swelling

Some swelling and discomfort for 2–3 days after placement is normal. Cold compresses and prescribed medicines manage this easily.

🥣

Soft Diet Initially

Stick to soft foods for the first week while healing. Gradually return to all foods once your dentist gives the go-ahead.

🪥

Oral Hygiene

Brush gently twice daily, use interdental brushes around the implant, and rinse with antibacterial mouthwash as advised.

📅

Regular Check-ups

Follow-up visits at 1 week, 1 month, and every 6 months thereafter ensure your implant remains in perfect condition.
